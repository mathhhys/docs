## Why Profiles Matter

- Quickly toggle between providers without reconfiguring settings each time
- Experiment with different models for specific tasks
- Save and reuse multiple optimized setups (e.g., ‚ÄúFast Debugging‚Äù vs. ‚ÄúDeep Reasoning‚Äù)

## What Profiles Can Include

Each profile stores:

- **API Providers** ‚Üí OpenAI, Anthropic, OpenRouter, Glama, etc.
- **API Keys & Auth** ‚Üí Securely stored credentials per provider
- **Model Selection** ‚Üí Choose from models like _o3-mini-high_, _Claude 3.7 Sonnet_, _DeepSeek R1_
- **Model Parameters** ‚Üí Adjust temperature, max tokens, or provider-specific options
- **Thinking Budgets** ‚Üí Control depth of reasoning where supported
- **Provider-Specific Features** ‚Üí Settings unique to certain providers/models

## Creating & Managing Profiles

### **1. Creating a Profile**

1. Open **Settings ‚Üí Providers**
2. Click the **‚Äú+‚Äù button** next to the profile selector
3. Enter a **name** for your new profile
4. Configure:
   - Select an **API provider**
   - Enter your **API key**
   - Pick a **model**
   - Adjust **parameters** (temperature, tokens, etc.)

### **2. Switching Profiles**

- **From Settings** ‚Üí Use the profile dropdown to switch configurations
- **During Chat** ‚Üí Use the **API Configuration dropdown** in the chat panel

### **3. Pinning & Sorting Profiles**

- Hover over a profile in the dropdown ‚Üí click the **pin icon**
- Pinned profiles appear at the **top** (alphabetical order)
- Unpinned profiles appear **below a separator** (also alphabetical)
- Click the pin again to unpin

üëâ Perfect for quickly jumping between your most-used profiles.

### **4. Editing & Deleting Profiles**

- Select the profile in **Settings** to update details
- Rename ‚Üí Click the **pencil icon**
- Delete ‚Üí Click the **trash icon** (cannot delete your last remaining profile)

## Linking Profiles to Modes

In the **Prompts tab**, you can:

- Associate specific profiles with different **Modes** (e.g., Chat, Code Generation, Debugging)
- The system automatically remembers which profile you last used with each mode

‚û°Ô∏è This ensures smoother, **mode-specific workflows** without constant switching.
